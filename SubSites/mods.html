Installation Instructions:

  1. Ensure you have the latest version of Minecraft Forge installed for 1.20.1.
  2. Download the provided zip file.
  3. In the main menu of the game, click the "Mods" button.
  4. Open the mods folder.
  5. Locate and left-click the ".minecraft" and "mods" folders.
  6. Copy the address of the "mods" folder.
  7. Unzip the downloaded zip file.
  8. When prompted for the extraction destination, paste the copied address into the address bar.
  9. Complete the extraction process.
  10. Enjoy your newly installed mods!
